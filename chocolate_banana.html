Ingredients

Method

In a blender, combine the frozen banana, milk of your choice, penut butter, protein poweder and unsweetened cocoa powder. Blend until thoroughly combined and no chunks of banana remain. If you find the smoothie a bit too thick, you can add in a bit more milk until you reach your desired consistency.

Pour into a glass and serve as desired. Enjoy!
